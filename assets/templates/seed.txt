# Seed concept

A good seed has FOUR things: a world-differentiator, a central tension, a cost/constraint, and a sensory/human hook. Reject generic fare (chosen-one, dark lord, medieval-Europe-plus-elves for fiction; "mindfulness will fix you" for self-help).
