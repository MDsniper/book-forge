# Curated by book-forge from public prose-craft criticism and AI-writing
# detection literature (Pangram's published research, GPTZero's burstiness/
# perplexity methodology, the Editorial Freelancers Association's style
# guidance, and writing-craft literature on filter words and telling verbs).
# Patterns target the same *categories* of AI tells recognized industry-wide
# but the regexes and detection language are book-forge's own.
# slop_scan.py compiles each line as a regex with re.IGNORECASE.
#
# One regex per line. Blank lines and lines starting with # are ignored.
